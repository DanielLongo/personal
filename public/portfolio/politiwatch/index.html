Politiwatch


501(c)(3) nonprofit

F ’16 – politics oversight

Politiwatch is a non-profit that technology to promote political accountability, digital rights, and social awareness. I founded Politiwatch as a way to distance myself from some of my more controversial projects. (They weren’t that controversial, but I didn’t feel comfortable putting my name on them.)

If you find the work I do as Politiwatch impactful, please consider donating.

Politiwatch’s sponsors include Google (via their Google for Nonprofits program), Microsoft (though their nonprofit grants program), ProtonMail, and DigitalOcean.
