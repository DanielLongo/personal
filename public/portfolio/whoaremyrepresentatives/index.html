WhoAreMyRepresentatives


Easily find your reps

W ’16 – politics civics politiwatch

WhoAreMyRepresentatives.org (WAMR) is a simple tool to look up the government representatives for any U.S. address. It pulls its information from the Google Civics API and the OpenSecrets API.

  • Used more than 1 million times and maintains steady traffic.
  • Displayed in the top spot of Hacker News for over nine hours.
  • Featured on ProductHunt.
  • Hit the front page of Reddit (5k+ upvotes).
  • Received an (informal) invitation to visit Google.
  • Shared by Dave Lee, Colin Wright, HANA, Mike Elgan, and others.
  • Featured in BoingBoing, Bustle, TimeOut (twice), & others.
  • Linked to as an activism resource by Planned Parenthood.
